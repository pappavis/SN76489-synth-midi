# PSG_SN76489.sch — PCF8574(AP) → SN76489 + audio out

### SN76489 pinout (DIP-16, klassiek)
        SN76489 (DIP-16)
      ┌─────────────────┐
 D0 ──│ 1            16 │── NC
 D1 ──│ 2            15 │── NC
 D2 ──│ 3            14 │── GND_DIG
 D3 ──│ 4            13 │── +5V_DIG
 D4 ──│ 5            12 │── PSG_AUDIO_RAW
 D5 ──│ 6            11 │── CE
 D6 ──│ 7            10 │── CLK
 D7 ──│ 8             9 │── WE
      └─────────────────┘

### Verbindinen (essentieel)
D0..D7  (SN pins 1..8)  ← PCF P0..P7
WE      (SN pin 9)      ← PCF WE_STROBE
CLK     (SN pin 10)     ← SN_CLK (extern of module-clock)
CE      (SN pin 11)     → GND_DIG  (V1: altijd enabled)
AUDIO   (SN pin 12)     → PSG_AUDIO_RAW (naar audio sheet)
+5V     (SN pin 13)     → +5V_DIG
GND     (SN pin 14)     → GND_DIG

### Decoupling (verplicht, fysiek dicht bij SN)
+5V_DIG ──|| C1 100n ── GND_DIG
+5V_DIG ──|| C2 10u  ── GND_DIG
